Lupita

There's something about seeing african culture on the big screen - its almost magical.

Lupita is a result of the excitement that came after watching Black Panther. I have never seen blackness celebrated on such a large scale. Our culture wasn't being exploited, appropiated or speculated. As well as finding inspiration in art, I also find inspiration in such visuals.

So I want this section to be a source of visual inspiration; I will be updating it regularly with videos that currently inspire or bring joy. If you ever need to get away from your thoughts, or life is trying you, you can come to this page.


Michaela Coel in conversation. This video is inspiring to me because she speaks very eloquently.




Childish Gambino, So into you cover. This video brings me joy, I always find myself going back to it.




Choergraphy piece by Will West. This video brings joy because it is pure, black artistry.




ChloexHalle, Do it. Two sisters, in their element.




Kojey Radical, 700 Pennies. A very underated artist, his videos are both emotive and complete bodies of work.




J cole in Conversation. This video is inspiring because he makes some good points to think about.
